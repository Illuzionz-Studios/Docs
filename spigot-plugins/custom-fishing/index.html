    Custom Fishing

    Custom Fishing is a plugin designed to overhaul the fishing system in minecraft. It adds all new loot making fishing a more thrilling experience than just fish and the occasional treasure. Custom Fishing supports unlimited custom rewards with lots of cosmetics features such as titles, sounds, and lots of options to restrict where you can find certain items. This plugin is actively being worked on and more features are to come.

    Take your time to read through this wiki to understand how the plugin works and solve any problems you may encounter
